Preguntas Frecuentes

¿How can I place an order?

You can place an order through our website. Simply select the products you want, add them to your shopping cart, and proceed to checkout with the available options.

¿What are the payment options?

We accept payments by credit card, PayPal and bank transfers. We also offer payment options through MercadoLibre and other methods available on our website.

¿What countries do you ship to?

We currently ship to all of Argentina. For international shipping, please contact us directly for more information.

¿How long does shipping take?

Delivery time varies by location, but generally shipping can take between 3 and 7 business days within Argentina. International shipments may take longer, depending on the destination.

¿Can I return a product?

Yes, we offer a return policy within 15 days of purchase as long as the product is unused and in its original condition. Please see our return policies for more details.

¿Are the products of quality?

All of our products are made with high quality materials, carefully selected to ensure durability and beauty. We offer a limited warranty to ensure our customers' satisfaction.

¿How can I know my size?

In each product, we provide a size guide to help you choose the correct size. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact us and we will be happy to assist you.

¿Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes, we regularly offer discounts and special promotions. You can subscribe to our newsletter to stay up to date with the latest offers.

¿Can I customize a product?

Yeah! We offer customization options for some products. If you are interested in customizing an item, please contact us for more details.

¿What do I do if my product arrives damaged or defective?

We regret any inconvenience. If your product arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately to arrange a return or replacement.

Can I change my order once it has been placed?

If you need to change anything about your order, please let us know as soon as possible. If the order has not yet been processed or shipped, we can make changes without problem.

¿How should I care for my jewelry products?

To ensure the durability of your jewelry, we recommend that you do not expose it to chemicals, perfumes, or humidity. Store your pieces in a dry place and avoid constant contact with water.

¿Do you have a physical store?

We currently do not have a physical store, but you can buy all our products online. If you need assistance, we are available to help you through our customer service.

¿How can I contact you?

You can contact us through our email [correo@bijouterie.com] or send us a message through our social networks. We are here to help you with any questions you have.
